Avatune is a toolkit for building customizable avatars. Pick a renderer for your framework, choose a theme you like, and you're good to go.

## What's in here

- **Getting Started** — Install a renderer and render your first avatar in under a minute.
- **Playground** — Try different themes and tweak avatar options live.
- **Frameworks** — React, React Native, Vue, Svelte, Vanilla JS. Same API everywhere.
- **Themes** — Different art styles to pick from. Each one lists what parts you can customize.
- **Predictors** — ML models that look at a photo and suggest hair color, hair length, or skin tone for your avatar.
- **API** — Generate avatars on the server if you don't want to bundle themes client-side.

## Where to start

New here? [Getting Started](/getting-started/) will get you up and running fast.

Want to explore? Jump into the [Playground](/playground/) and see what's possible.
